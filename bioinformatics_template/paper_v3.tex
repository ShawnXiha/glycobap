\begin{abstract}
\noindent\textbf{Motivation:} AlphaFold 3 has revolutionized protein structure prediction but standard input formats produce stereochemically incorrect glycan structures.

\noindent\textbf{Results:} We present GlycoSMILES2BAP achieving 97.8\% epimer accuracy, 97.4\% anomeric accuracy, and 95.9\% linkage accuracy.
